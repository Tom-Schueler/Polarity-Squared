# Polarity-Squared

is the project I worked on during the first semester of my education at the School for Games (S4G) in collaboration with 6 of my fellow students -_you can find their names on the itch page_. Using Godot Engine was a requirement as per the S4G's curriculum.

It is a steampunk-inspired 2D platformer where you have to make precise and timely jumps using gravity manipulation to avoid obstacles and reach the goal.
